About Twin Primes

The Conjecture

The Twin Prime Conjecture states that there are infinitely many pairs of prime numbers that differ by 2 (e.g., 11 and 13, 17 and 19).

The Challenge

While mathematicians believe it's true, it hasn't been proven. We can search for new record-breaking twin prime pairs!

Big Integer Support

This explorer uses Big Integer arithmetic to search for twin primes with hundreds of digits, far beyond JavaScript's number limits.
