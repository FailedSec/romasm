About Perfect Numbers

Definition

A perfect number is a positive integer that equals the sum of its proper divisors (excluding itself).

Example: 6
Divisors: 1, 2, 3
Sum: 1 + 2 + 3 = 6 ‚úì

The Mystery

All known perfect numbers are even. No odd perfect number has ever been found, despite centuries of searching. This is one of the oldest unsolved problems in mathematics.

The Challenge

Use Romasm to test vast ranges of odd numbers, checking if any satisfy the perfect number property. Finding one would be a major mathematical discovery!
